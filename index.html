About Me

My all-time favorite book is "The Little Prince". It was a lighthearted read for when I was little, and I still revisit it every now and again to see how it changed its meaning. Cliche as it might be, one or my favorite quotes in the book is

“All grown-ups were once children... but only few of them remember it.”

I realised only recently the deeper meaning this book holds, but, for now, I'd like to focus on the importance of this particurlar quote for me. While the book can be interpreted in many different ways, for me, it's about growing up and going into 'real life'.
I feel like, for me, that might happen all to soon.
So, ultimately, my goal in life is to keep my childish spirit, however messy it might be, because, thanks to it, I developed a deep love for the arts, which I will pursue further. And if I have done nothing else than make my child self happy, I will have lived the very best life.
